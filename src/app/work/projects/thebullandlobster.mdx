## Overview

The Bull & Lobster is a restaurant site for a UK brand, showcasing the menu, ambience, and booking touchpoints in a single experience.

## Key Features

- **Menu & specials**: Structured menu sections that highlight seasonal dishes.
- **Bookings & enquiries**: Reservation and contact capture handled via the ExpressJS backend.
- **Location & hours**: Clear venue details with calls to action for visits and events.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**.
- **ExpressJS**.
- **ShadCN UI**.
- **Typescript**.
